Spirits

Illinois

WOOD SPECIES: WHITE OAK (Quercus Alba)
MASH BILL: 72% CORN, 23% RYE, 5% BARLEY
TASTING NOTES: BAKING SPICES, CARAMEL CORN, GOLDEN RAISINS, TOAST, CAMPFIRE

Illinois is one of the most fertile places on the planet.
Before European settlers and their plows came west, prairies extended for thousands of unbroken miles. Grasses sent roots deep into glacial deposits and over millennia created rich topsoil several feet deep. Oak savannas punctuated the prairie landscape and provided respite for bison, elk and deer and the indigenous tribes that would use controlled fires to maintain these biodiverse areas. Split Spirits combines this iconic tree with Illinois corn, rye and barley to create a spirit rooted in Illinois.

THE CORN CRIB
2 parts IL White Oak
½ part Lemon Juice
¼ part Simple Syrup
1 egg white
Dry shake then shake with ice. Strain into chilled glass. Garnish with cherry.
